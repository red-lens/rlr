Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Overview

RedLens Recorder (RLR) is a privacy-focused, disability-assistive audio and video recording application designed to operate primarily offline.

We do NOT collect, store, transmit, or sell personal data. All recordings remain on your device unless you choose to share them.

Permissions and Why They Are Required

  • Camera & Microphone — required to capture video and audio that you explicitly start.
  • Storage / Media Access — required to save recordings locally so you can view, play, or delete them.
  • Foreground Service & Notifications — required by Android to ensure you are always aware when recording is active.

Permissions are used strictly for core functionality and never for advertising, analytics, profiling, or tracking.

AccessibilityService Disclosure (Optional Feature)

RedLens Recorder includes an optional AccessibilityService feature designed exclusively to assist users who may have difficulty interacting with the screen.

Purpose: The service allows users to start or stop recording by pressing hardware volume buttons, providing a hands-free shortcut for accessibility needs.

The service is NOT used to:

  • Read screen content
  • Monitor user activity
  • Capture typed text or passwords
  • Intercept communications
  • Perform gestures or actions on your behalf
  • Modify system settings

This feature is disabled by default and requires:

  • Explicit in-app consent
  • Manual activation in Android Accessibility Settings

You may disable it at any time from your device settings.

Data Collection

We do not collect personal information.

  • No account creation
  • No cloud storage
  • No analytics SDKs
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No background data transmission

Data Storage and Retention

  • All recordings are stored locally on your device.
  • Files remain until you delete them.
  • The developer cannot access your recordings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, transfer, or share your data with third parties.

Any sharing of recordings occurs only if you explicitly choose to export or send a file.

User Awareness During Recording

To prevent unintended recording:

  • A persistent notification is displayed while recording.
  • Foreground service indicators remain visible.
  • Recording only begins after a clear user action.

Security

Your recordings benefit from Android platform security protections, including sandboxing, device encryption, and permission controls.

You maintain full control over app permissions and stored media.

Children’s Privacy

This application is not directed toward children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the app indicates acceptance of the latest version.
