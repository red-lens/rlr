Privacy Policy

Last updated: [November 22, 2025]

1. Overview

RedLens Recorder is a personal camera and microphone recording app designed to work fully offline. The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with the developer or third parties.

2. Permissions Used

Permissions are used only for core functionality:

  • Camera – capture video
  • Microphone – record audio
  • Foreground Service – keep recording active with a visible notification
  • Notifications – show ongoing recording status
  • Storage / Media Access – save and manage recordings locally

Never used for tracking, analytics, monitoring, or hidden behavior.

3. Data Collection & Storage

  • Recordings are stored only on your device
  • No automatic uploads or syncing
  • The developer has zero access to your files

4. Data Sharing

The App does not share data with third parties and contains no advertising, analytics services, or external tracking.

5. User Control

  • Start and stop recording at any time
  • View and delete recordings inside the App
  • Uninstalling keeps your control over stored files
  • A visible notification is always shown while recording

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 13 and collects no data from children.

7. Security

Recordings remain protected by your device’s security features (screen lock, encryption, and physical access). The App does not bypass or weaken system protection.

8. Changes to This Policy

Updates may occur over time. Continued use indicates acceptance of the latest version of this policy.
